Ory introduced Ory Talos, a platform that replaces traditional API keys with dynamic, revocable credentials to secure AI agents and non-human identities in enterprise networks. The solution uses Macaroon-based delegation and token derivation to provide fine-grained access control with short expiration windows. According to research, while over 80% of organizations have deployed AI agents in production, only 21% have documented governance policies in place.
