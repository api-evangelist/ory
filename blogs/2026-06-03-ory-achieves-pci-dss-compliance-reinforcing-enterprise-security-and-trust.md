Ory has achieved PCI DSS compliance for service providers, validated by BARR Advisory, demonstrating adherence to rigorous security standards for protecting sensitive data. The certification strengthens Ory's broader security program, which also incorporates SOC 2 Type II controls, ISO 27001 alignment, and GDPR readiness. This milestone reinforces customer confidence in Ory's identity management platform, which serves enterprise clients across multiple industries.
