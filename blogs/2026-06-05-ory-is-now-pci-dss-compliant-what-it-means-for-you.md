Ory has achieved PCI DSS SAQ D compliance for service providers, assessed by BARR Advisory, demonstrating that Ory's identity infrastructure meets stringent security standards. The certification enables customers to streamline their own compliance audits by leveraging Ory's validated controls. The company employs advanced encryption, strict access controls, and continuous monitoring to protect identity data in applications handling payments.
